A bit of history

A long time ago...

The UniCariocaDev Group was created back in 2011.

Our motivation is to connect people and help each other to evolve, personally and professionally. During the years, we saw students coming to the market lacking some important skills. They didn't know the main technologies that the companies working on. So the Matrix Project was born. The Matrix's purpose is helping the students to improve their skills. We've used an immersive approach, giving them a full experience software development scenario, like in a Startup.
